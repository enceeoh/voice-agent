# Fuseworks Electrical — Price Book (FW-2026-03)

## 1. Call-out and Labour Rates

- Standard labour: £68/hour, one-hour minimum.
- Emergency call-out (same day, evenings, weekends): £120 first hour, then £90/hour.
- Free site survey for any quoted job over £500. Surveys for smaller jobs: £45, deducted from the final invoice if the job goes ahead.

## 2. EV Charger Installation

- Standard 7kW wall-mounted charger, supplied and installed: £1,050 (tethered) or £980 (untethered).
- Customer-supplied charger, installation only: £420.
- Cable run over 10 metres from consumer unit to charge point: add £12 per additional metre.
- 22kW three-phase charger: £1,850 supplied and installed — only where a three-phase supply already exists. Single-to-three-phase upgrades are quoted separately via the DNO and typically exceed £4,000.
- All EV charger installations require notification to the DNO under G98/G99 — included in our price.

## 3. Solar PV Installation

- 4-panel system (approx 1.8kWp) with hybrid inverter: £4,200.
- 8-panel system (approx 3.6kWp) with hybrid inverter: £6,400.
- 12-panel system (approx 5.4kWp) with hybrid inverter: £8,900.
- Battery storage add-on (5kWh): £2,750 fitted at the same time as panels; £3,100 if retrofitted later.
- Scaffolding: £650–£900 depending on roof height and access; always required, always itemised separately.
- Bird-proofing mesh: £320 per array (optional).
- In-roof (integrated) panels instead of on-roof mounting: add 15% to the system price.

## 4. Consumer Units and Inspections

- Consumer unit (fuse box) replacement with modern RCBO board: £580 for up to 10 circuits, £720 for 11–16 circuits.
- EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report): £180 for properties up to 4 bedrooms, £240 for larger.
- Remedial work identified by an EICR is quoted separately after the report.

## 5. Rewires and Small Works

- Full rewire: £1,150 per bedroom (e.g. 3-bed house ≈ £3,450), excluding redecorating.
- Additional double socket on existing ring: £95 first, £55 each thereafter in the same visit.
- Outdoor weatherproof socket: £140.
- Light fitting replacement: £45 each; new lighting circuit: £280.
- Electric shower circuit (10.5kW): £340.

## 6. Bundle Pricing

- EV charger + solar installed together: 5% off the combined price.
- Solar + battery + EV charger ("whole-home package"): 8% off the combined price, and the survey fee is always waived.
